Summit Technology Academy


I began learning programming in highschool when I attended the Summit Technology Academy in Lees Summit, Missouri. My class at STA was a daily two hour long block class where for the first semester we learned C++ and the fundamentals of object oriented programming, and the second semester was spent learning Java.
I think going to Summit Technology Academy was a great formative experience because the longer structured classes allowed for a much more in-depth education than I think normal highschool classes could have offered, which led to a well rounded introduction to the field of computer science and prepared me extraordinarilly well for college.


Missouri University of Science and Technology

After highschool I went to college at Missouri University of Science and Technology in Rolla, Missouri where I got my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics. My coursework in college mainly involved writing code in python. I also learned the basics of writing assembly code through my various computer engineering courses.

Some of my coursework included:

  • Data Structures
  • File Structures and Databases
  • Theory of Computer Science
  • Computer Networks
  • Computer Security
  • Evolutionary Computing
  • Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
  • Introduction to Deep Learning
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Microcontrollers and Embedded Systems


I also participated in Capstone I and II, classes that each involved a semester long group project. In Capstone I, my group and I designed an implementation of scrabble using Unreal Engine 4.
